About Pehchaan

Inclusive fashion, made properly

Pehchaan is an inclusive fashion brand built for people who are often left out of the mainstream fashion system. We focus on designing garments for blind users, wheelchair users, and individuals with mobility limitations, giving them stylish clothing that feels good and fits their lifestyle.

Who We Are

Pehchaan is an inclusive fashion brand built for people who are often left out of the mainstream fashion system. We focus on designing garments for blind users, wheelchair users, and individuals with mobility limitations, giving them stylish clothing that feels good and fits their lifestyle.

What the Brand Stands For

We stand for fashion that is accessible, respectful, and made for real needs. Pehchaan represents identity, empowerment, and equal space in clothing for people with disabilities.

Why We Created Pehchaan

Pehchaan was created because disabled individuals struggle to find clothes made for their comfort, style, and needs. No mainstream brand offers proper adaptive options, so we built a brand where they can finally find clothing that understands them.

What We Offer

We design adaptive clothing that prioritizes comfort, dignity and independence. Our garments feature easy openings, soft seams, tactile guides and accessible closures to support smooth daily dressing. Each piece is crafted with breathable, durable fabrics selected for sensitivity and long-term wear. We also offer customization based on individual needs, ensuring every garment feels personal and functional. Our brand stands out for its human-centered design approach, combining craftsmanship, accessibility and style in every piece.

Our Values

  • Inclusivity: Designing for all abilities, not just the able-bodied.
  • Dignity: Clothing that supports pride, confidence, and identity.
  • Comfort: Soft, breathable materials and easy-to-wear features.
  • Respect: Listening to users and designing from their lived experience.

Target Audience

Blind and Low-Vision Users
Clothing designed with tactile markers, intuitive layouts and sensory cues to support independent dressing.
Wheelchair Users
Adaptive cuts, pressure-free seams and seated-fit designs that offer comfort without compromising style.
People With Mobility Limitations
Easy-open closures, flexible fits and minimal-effort dressing solutions for a smoother daily wear.
Caregivers or Families
Garments that simplify assisted dressing, reduce physical strain and provide a dignified experience for the wearer.
