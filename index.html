Who We Are

Pehchaan is an inclusive fashion brand built for people who are often left out of the mainstream fashion system. We design garments for blind users, wheelchair users, and individuals with mobility limitations, giving them stylish clothing that feels good and fits their lifestyle.

At Pehchaan, we believe everyone deserves fashion that represents who they are.

Why We Created Pehchaan

Disabled individuals often struggle to find clothes made for their comfort, style, and needs. Mainstream brands rarely offer proper adaptive options, and getting dressed can become tiring, painful, or impossible without help.

Pehchaan was created to change that – a brand where adaptive clothing is designed first, not added later.

Users & Their Outfits

We designed four outfits for four different users. Each look focuses on comfort, independence, and style for their specific needs.

Blind / Low-vision User

Needs clear tactile feedback, easy closures, and simple coordination of colors.

  • Tactile tags on inside seams to identify front/back.
  • Contrasting piping that can be felt by touch.
  • Magnetic buttons for easy and safe closure.

Wheelchair User

Needs comfort in sitting position, no pressure points, and easy access for dressing.

  • Higher back rise and lower front waist for seated comfort.
  • Side zippers in pants for easier dressing.
  • Seam placement away from pressure areas.

User with Leg Support Needs

Needs flexible fit around legs and easy openings for braces or medical support.

  • Adjustable straps and openings around knees/ankles.
  • Stretch panels for comfortable movement.
  • Hidden zippers to wear over braces or supports.

Older Adult with Mobility Limitations

Needs simple, low-effort dressing and soft fabrics that do not irritate the skin.

  • Wide openings at neck and sleeves.
  • Velcro or snap closures instead of small buttons.
  • Soft, warm fabric suitable for winter.
